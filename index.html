You Have Questions, We Have the Answers!

Why are you running?

Albertans deserve leaders who put people before politics. My campaign is built on accountability, transparency, and respect for all people.

What are your top priorities?

Accountability & Transparency. Responsible Growth, and Stronger Communities.

Which constituency are you running in?

I am preparing to run provincially in Alberta, with my current focus including Edmonton - Gleonra. I am listening to communities across Alberta to determine where I can best serve.

Will you work with other parties or candidates?

I am independent, but I believe in collaboration. I will work with anyone who rejects fear-based control and shares the goal of building a more accountable, transparent, and respectful Alberta.

How will you fund your campaign?

Until the writ drops, I can only accept in-kind contributions. Once donations are permitted, I will be able to accept financial contributions in full compliance with Elections Alberta rules and regulations.

How can I support your campaign right now?

You can pledge to donate, contribute in-kind, or sign up to volunteer.

What makes you different from other candidates?

I am not here to split the vote or recycle old party politics. I am here to challenge entrenched Conservative dominance and offer Albertans a real alternative.

How will you ensure accountability if elected?

I will publish clear and measurable goals and report progress regularly.

What values guide your decisions?

Respect, Accountability, and Transparency

What's your vision for Alberta?

An Alberta where government is accountable, communities are strong, and growth benefits for everyone -- not just a few.
